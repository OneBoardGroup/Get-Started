
## Preparation Questions

**Oneboard is designed to operate across multiple environments and can be adapted to different organizational, technical, and regulatory setups.**  
The following questions are intended to support internal preparation for the Oneboard implementation and to ensure a smooth and efficient rollout.

If any questions arise during this process, please reach out directly to:

**[bh@cll.li](mailto:bh@cll.li)**

### Technical & Organizational Clarifications

- Will Oneboard be operated **on-premise** or in the **cloud**?
        
- Are there any **firewall restrictions** or network policies that must be considered?
    
- Are **IMAP / SMTP** services available and approved for system-generated emails?
    
- Which **email address or domain** should be used for client communication?
    
- Is the use of **Docker** intendet?
    
- What **backup processes** are in place or required (frequency, retention, responsibility)?
    
- Are there any **critical interfaces to internal systems or processes** (e.g. CRM, core banking, compliance, archiving)?
